Title: Joint Stipulation to Dismiss with Prejudice
Date: 2021-03-01
Chunks: 1

================================================================================

On March 1, 2021, the plaintiffs Robert Martin, Pamela Hawkes, and Robert Anderson filed a Joint Stipulation to Dismiss with Prejudice in the case against the City of Boise, identified as Case No. 1:09-cv-00540-REB [CITE:doc_154972_chunk_00000]. The parties reached a settlement regarding the claims in this action, as detailed in their Joint Motion for Dismissal with Prejudice and accompanying documents [CITE:doc_154972_chunk_00001]. 

The stipulation indicates that all claims in the action will be dismissed with prejudice, meaning they cannot be refiled in the future. Each party has agreed to bear its own costs, expenses, and attorneys' fees, unless otherwise specified in the Settlement Agreement [CITE:doc_154972_chunk_00001]. 

The document was signed by multiple attorneys representing both the plaintiffs and the defendants, including Howard A. Belodoff from Idaho Legal Aid Services, Michael E. Bern and others from Latham & Watkins LLP for the plaintiffs, and Jayme Sullivan and Scott Muir from the Boise City Attorney’s Office for the defendants [CITE:doc_154972_chunk_00002]. 

Additionally, a Certificate of Service was included, certifying that the stipulation was electronically filed and served to the relevant parties on the same date [CITE:doc_154972_chunk_00003].
