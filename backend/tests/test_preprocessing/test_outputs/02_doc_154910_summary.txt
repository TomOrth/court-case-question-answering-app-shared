================================================================================

The United States District Court for the District of Idaho issued an Order of Dismissal with Prejudice on March 2, 2021, regarding the case Robert Martin, et al. v. City of Boise, et al., Case No. 1:09-cv-00540-REB. The dismissal was based on a Joint Stipulation to Dismiss with Prejudice filed by the parties (Dkt. 392), in accordance with FRCP 41(a)(1)(A)(ii) of the Federal Rules of Civil Procedure. The Court ordered that the action be dismissed in its entirety and directed the Clerk’s Office to close the case [CITE:doc_154910_chunk_00000]. The order was signed by Chief U.S. Magistrate Judge Ronald E. Bush.
